Commemorative postage stamps and initiatives undertaken within the framework of the "Heydar Aliyev Year" in honor of the 100th anniversary of the birth of the Great Leader Heydar Aliyev.

Within the framework of the 'Heydar Aliyev Year,' postage stamps dedicated to the 100th anniversary of the birth of the Great Leader Heydar Aliyev, have been released by 'Azerpost' LLC, which is under the Ministry of Digital Development and Transport.

Commemorative stamps dedicated to the memory of the founder of the independent Azerbaijan state, the brilliant politician, and powerful statesman Heydar Aliyev have been prepared in various categories and printed in two countries.

Postage stamps

Among these stamps, there is a limited edition block consisting of 1 stamp only, with a total circulation of 2000, each uniquely numbered. This special block has been printed at the "Bundesdruckerei" publishing house in Berlin, Germany. Special attention has been paid to the design of the stamp. Specifically, the block features a photo of Heydar Aliyev and his famous quote "I have always been proud, and today I am proud to be Azerbaijani" in golden foil. The nominal value of the stamp is set at 10 manat.


Additionally, for the first time in Azerbaijan, a postage stamp with a nominal value of 0.50 qəpik and featuring a "QR code" has been printed in Germany with a circulation of 300,000 and released. This stamp aims to facilitate widespread access to information about the Great Leader among international audiences. The QR code directs to information about Heydar Aliyev in English on the president.az website.


The other printed stamps are prepared in block and miniature sheet formats. Designed in a contemporary style, these postage stamps feature various photos of the National Leader taken at different times.


At a time when interest in Azerbaijani stamps is once again on the rise worldwide, the release of these stamps not only serves as a significant gift for collectors in the philatelic world but also promotes our country internationally by being affixed to letters and spread worldwide. This is precisely why "Azerpost" LLC has produced commemorative stamps for "Heydar Aliyev 100 years," featuring the logo, with 6 different denominations and a total circulation of 4,557,000 standard postage stamps, along with stamped envelopes. These items have been released for domestic and international postal use. The standard postage stamps have been printed at the "Integrated Printing House" in the city of Bobruysk, Belarus.


As a result of the initiative by the Ministry of Digital Development and Transport within the framework of the "Heydar Aliyev Year" and through the close collaboration of "Azerpost" LLC, postage stamps commemorating the 100th anniversary of the birth of the Great Leader Heydar Aliyev have also been issued by other countries.
